##AIM
To explore the dynamics of coexistence of native vs exotic communities in disturbed dendritic networks:

What determines coexistence of native fish species within a "Pristine" Mediterranean (environmentally constrained) dendritic network? How is the community dynamics and -ultimately - composition affected by the introduction of dams (large obstacles to connectivity) and exotic species? Does the native community change when dams and/or exotics are introduced? What is the probability of extinction  of native species after exotics have been introduced, and what (local) factors can increase that probability?
Asymmetric basin (current species assemblages are very different in right vs. left hand margin), does it play a role in the dynamics of coexistence?
